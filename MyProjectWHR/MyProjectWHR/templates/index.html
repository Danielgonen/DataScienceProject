World Happiness Report

Daniel's Flask Project

What is it?

The World Happiness Report is a landmark survey of the state of global happiness that ranks 156 countries by how happy their citizens perceive themselves to be.

Why I chose this topic?

In my idea, it's interesting to know what countries or regions rank the highest in overall happiness or did any country experience a significant increase or decrease in happiness between the years 2016, 2018 and 2019?

My sources
